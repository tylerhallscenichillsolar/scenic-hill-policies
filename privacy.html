Privacy Policy — Internal Finance Automation

Last updated: August 08, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Scenic Hill Solar (“Company”) processes data in its internal finance automation application (“Application”). The Application is for internal business use only.

1. Data We Process

  • Financial records from QuickBooks Online (e.g., chart of accounts, GL detail, AR/AP, balance sheet, vendor/customer names).
  • Operational metadata (timestamps, job run status, system logs).
  • User identifiers limited to Company accounts used to access the Application.

2. Purpose of Processing

  • Financial reporting, reconciliation, variance analysis, and preparation of cleanup recommendations.
  • Internal auditability and troubleshooting (system logs).

3. Sharing & Third Parties

  • QuickBooks Online API for read‑only access to Company financial data.
  • AI model provider for analytical processing; data sent is limited to what is required for the task. Outputs are reviewed by finance staff before action.
  • No sale of data. No disclosure to external parties except service providers acting on our instructions.

4. Data Security

  • Access controlled via least‑privilege principles and company SSO where applicable.
  • Secrets (e.g., tokens) stored in encrypted vaults; transport protected with TLS.
  • System logs maintained for audit trail and anomaly detection.

5. Data Retention

  • Operational logs retained per Company policy (e.g., 90–365 days).
  • Generated reports retained in Company storage per finance record retention schedules.

6. User Choices & Responsibilities

  • Only authorized personnel may use the Application.
  • Users must not upload or include sensitive personal data unless required for a documented business purpose.

7. International Transfers

If service providers process data outside your jurisdiction, Company ensures appropriate safeguards consistent with Company policy and applicable law.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via internal channels.

9. Contact

Questions about this Policy: privacy@scenichillsolar.com

This policy is intended for internal informational purposes and is not legal advice. Company counsel should review before external distribution.
